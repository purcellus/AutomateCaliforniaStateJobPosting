Department Information

THE MISSION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF HOUSING AND COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT (HCD) IS To provide leadership, policies and programs to preserve and expand safe and affordable housing opportunities and promote strong communities for all Californians.

We are located in a state of the art 'green' building located at 2020 West El Camino (directly off of I-5) with many close by amenities and free parking!


Department Website: http://www.hcd.ca.gov/

Job Description and Duties

The Information Technology Branch, Technical Support Section is looking for a motivated Associate Information Systems Analyst to work in the Help Desk Unit.

Under the supervision of the Staff Information Systems Analyst Supervisor the Associate Information Systems Analyst will assist clients with technical related issues and manage various servers in virtual and physical. This position will consist of responding to telephone and e-mail help desk requests concerning PC hardware and other computer related peripherals, Microsoft Windows 10 Desktop Operating System, Microsoft Office 2016 desktop applications, Track-It ticketing system, Citrix receiver - clients, troubleshooting data network connectivity issues, and set up hosted webinars for clients. The applicant will also be responsible for imaging and configuring PC's using Norton Ghost, implement encryption on mobile devices, and update and deploy Vipre anti-virus software. The applicant will develop client and technical procedures, will provide instructor led IT related training and assist with physical inventory.

**Please provide detailed and verifiable experience and/or verifiable proof of education such as transcripts that contain evidence of semester and/or quarter units in technology related coursework.**

Completed applications and all required documents must be received by the Final Filing Date in order to be considered.

Who May Apply

Completed applications and all required documents must be received or postmarked by the Final Filing Date in order to be considered. Individuals who are currently in the classification, eligible for lateral transfer, eligible for reinstatement, eligible for a Training and Development assignment, have SROA and/or Surplus eligibility, or have list eligibility (or are in the process of obtaining list eligibility). SROA and Surplus candidates may attach surplus letters to their applications. Applicants must meet any Minimum Qualifications stated in the Classification Specification(s). Applications will be screened and only the most qualified applicants will be selected for an interview.

How To Apply
